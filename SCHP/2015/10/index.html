2015年10月の記事一覧

54th IEEE CDC

  • 2015年10月 7日（水）

12月に大阪で開催される 54th IEEE Control Conference on Decision and Control (CDC 2015) に以下の3件が採択されました． 本カンファレンスでは，三平教授が Program Chair を務めております．

  • T. Ibuki, Y. Awai, Y. Sakayanagi, J. Kako and M. Sampei, "Knocking Detection in Gasoline Engines Based on Probability Density Functions: A Mixture Gaussian Distribution Approach", Proc. of the 54th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control, Osaka, Japan, to be presented, 2015
  • Y. Katsuyama, T. Ibuki and M. Sampei, "Attitude Controllability Analysis of an Underactuated Satellite with 2 Reaction Wheeles and Its Control", Proc. of the 54th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control, Osaka, Japan, to be presented, 2015
  • S. Kimura, H. Nakamura, T. Ibuki and M. Sampei, "Input Transformation with Coordinate Transformation for Nonlinear Systems subject to State Constraints", Proc. of the 54th IEEE Conference on Decision and Control, Osaka, Japan, to be presented, 2015
